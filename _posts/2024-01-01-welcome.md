This is your first post. It's a starting point for something new.

Writing on the internet has always felt like a peculiar act of faith. You sit down, organize your thoughts, and send them out into the void—hoping they might resonate with someone, somewhere.

## Why write?

There's something clarifying about putting thoughts into words. Ideas that seem brilliant in your head often reveal their rough edges when you try to explain them. Writing forces precision.

> The first draft is just you telling yourself the story.
> — Terry Pratchett

## What to expect

This space will evolve. Some posts will be technical, others more personal. The common thread is curiosity—an attempt to understand things a little better by writing about them.
